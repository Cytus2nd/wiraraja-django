    Why Us

    Wiraraja Pack is a manufacturer of plastics and stretch film located in Batam, Riau Islands, Indonesia. Our company has been partnering with experienced engineers and utilize highly curated materials and formulations to produce high performance plastics and stretch film products to provide the right packaging and packing solutions to fulfill your needs. Wiraraja Pack aims to be your trusted partner for bespoke business solutions across diverse sectors worldwide.
